Welcome to the Temple Photo Gallery

Temples are sacred places of worship for members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. They are houses of the Lord, where individuals make sacred covenants, receive eternal ordinances, and find peace and guidance through the Holy Spirit.

One beloved Primary song, I Love to See the Temple, teaches children the joy and anticipation of entering the temple. Music brings us closer to our Heavenly Father and helps us remember spiritual truths.
